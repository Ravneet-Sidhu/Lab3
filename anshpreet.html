SHRI DARBAR

Shri Darbar Sahib, also known as the Golden Temple, is the holiest Sikh shrine located in Amritsar. Its golden architecture and the serene Amrit Sarovar make it a symbol of spirituality and peace.

BHANGRA

Bhangra is Punjab’s signature dance, full of energy, jumps, and unstoppable beats. Once a way to celebrate harvests, it’s now a global dance sensation loved by all!

FARMING

Punjab is known as the "Granary of India," contributing significantly to the country's wheat and rice production. Its fertile lands and advanced irrigation systems make it a leader in agriculture.
